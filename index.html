a consistent maker of moves

Creative Artist and Social Entrepreneur

creativity has the power to transform...

Haylee Venus works in the realms of content creation, writing, poetry, design, dance and song. She aspires to serve the youth in the Caribbean and African continent, connecting with communities, producing workshops that educate, enlighten and entertain. Her ambition is to create a legacy that serves and preserves African literature, philosophy, culture and history, and to live, love and share all that is within her.
