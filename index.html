Author

A leading member of a number of social change projects and community-led enterprises. She established herself through a creative network, Embrace U Empire in 2012, and has since been developing herself as a Event Specialist, Community Organiser, Creative Artist and Social Entrepreneur.

This presenting her with many opportunities to partner with leading commercial and community organisations addressing homelessness, youth engagement & policy, celebration of arts and culture, health & wellbeing amongst many other initiatives.

Event Planning • Live Events • Writing • Public Speaking • Event Marketing • Content Strategy • Project Management • Blogging

In 2015, Haylee set up a Community Interest Company, Make A Step, which works internationally to build the confidence of 5-16 year olds through creative arts & sports volunteer programmes. The volunteer programmes are tailored to assist in the individual tapping into their full creative and sporting potential, stepping into their greatness, developing the skills that are necessary & transferable to their desired career paths and solving challenges within their communities. Haylee is aiming to increase the diaspora's involvement in community and social development across Africa and the Caribbean, and has already taken 34 volunteers on 13 international projects involving over 1500 young people in Kenya, Gambia, Jamaica and St Lucia in the last 7 years.
